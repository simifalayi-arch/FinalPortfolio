Swiss Design Style

The Swiss design style, also known as the International Typographic Style, emphasizes clarity, readability, and objectivity. It focuses on presenting information in a clean, structured, and visually balanced way.

Key Principles

  • Grid-based layouts
  • Sans-serif typography
  • Asymmetry
  • Clean visual hierarchy
  • Minimal decoration

Why This Style Matters

Swiss design is widely used in editorial layouts, corporate branding, and modern web design because it improves usability and communication. The emphasis on structure and simplicity makes content easy to scan and understand.
