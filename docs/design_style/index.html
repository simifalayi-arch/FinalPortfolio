Swiss Design Style

The Swiss (International Typographic) Style emphasizes clarity, objectivity, and readability through grid-based layouts and sans-serif typography.

Key Principles

  • Grid-based layout systems
  • Clean, sans-serif typography
  • Asymmetrical compositions
  • Minimal ornamentation

Why It Matters

Swiss design has influenced modern web, editorial, and interface design by prioritizing structure and usability. Its principles remain foundational in professional digital design.
